📷 Scanning Tips
☀️

Good Lighting

Scan near a bright window. Avoid shadows across the text.

📐

Flat Surface

Lay the document flat. Curved or folded paper reduces accuracy.

🔍

Fill the Frame

Get the document to fill most of your camera view.

🚫

No Glare

Tilt slightly to avoid shiny reflections on glossy paper.

📷

Hold Steady

Pause briefly after tapping — motion blur kills accuracy.

🔄

Retry if Needed

Not satisfied? Hit Clear and try again with better conditions.

🔄 How Sync Works
☁️

Real-Time Sync Between Both Phones

When either of you scans a document, it saves to your private Supabase database and appears on the other phone within seconds. Both of you sign in with your own email and password — only accounts you authorize can see your vault. The OCR runs entirely in your browser, so only the final text (not the image) is stored.
