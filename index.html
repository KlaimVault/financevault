📷 Scanning Tips for Best Results
☀️

Good Lighting

Scan near a bright window. Avoid shadows across the text.

📐

Lay It Flat

Place document flat on a surface. Curved paper reduces accuracy.

🔍

Fill the Frame

Get the document to fill most of your camera view.

🚫

Avoid Glare

Tilt slightly to avoid shiny reflections on glossy paper.

🤖

Claude AI Reads It

Claude AI extracts text, category, amount and due date automatically — much more accurate than standard OCR.

🔄

Retry if Needed

Not happy with results? Clear and try again with better lighting.
